About

LightDogs is a Web App aimed at Pet Product Companies. By correlating light levels and tweets using the word 'Dog', LightDogs can predict at what time marketing would be most effective for your business

The Project

This WebApp is the second part of Anna Bernbaum's Sensing and Internet of Things coursework. It was created between December 2018 and January 2019. The light data was collected usng a local sensor while the Twitter data was collected using an API.

Structure

The WebApp is structured as follows:
  • Timeline: This is a straightforward, interactive visualisation of the collected data.
  • Analysis: The collected data was statistically analysed. This may be of use to Pet Product Companies wishing to complete more in depth work.
  • Prediction: Want to know when to market your product? LightDogs will predict whether your selected time will be successful.
  • Insights: Some deeper insights can be drawn from looking at the other words in the Tweets, this page is a word cloud showing the most commonly used words.
